Role|
Dispatcher|
	* Move another player's pawn as if it were yours.

	* As an action, move any pawn to a city with another pawn.

	* Get permission before moving another player's pawn.|
Role|
Operations Expert|
	* As an action, build a research station in the city 
	  you are in (no City card needed).

	* Once per turn as an action, move from a research station 
          to any city by discarding any Citiy card.|
Role|
Scientist|
 	* You need only 4 cards of the same color to do the Discover a 
	  Cure action.|
Role|
Medic|
	* Remove all cubes of one color when doing Treat Disease.

	* Automatically remove cubes of cured diseases from the city you 
	  are in (and prevent them from being placed there).|
Role|
Researcher|
	* As an action, you may give (or a player can take) any City card 
	  from your hand.You must both be in the same city. The card does 
	  not have to match the city you are in.|
Role|
Contingency Planner|
	* As an action, take any discarded Event card and store it on this card.

	* When you play the stored Event card, remove it from the game.
	
	* Limit: 1 Event card on this card at a time, which is not part of 
	  your hand.|
Role|
Quarantine Specialist|
	* Prevent disease cube placements (and outbreaks) in the city you 
	  are in and all cities connected to it.|
